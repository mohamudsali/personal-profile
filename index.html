I am a Research Scientist at IBM Research in the Quantum Systems and Infrastructure team. I am currently reading my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering at The University of Minnesota-Twin Cities supervised by Prof. Kevin Cao. I hold MS in Electrical Engineering from Northwestern University-Evanston IL and BS in Electrical Engineering from The University of Minnesota-Twin Cities.

My research focuses on developing deployable tools with concrete methodologies and metrics to quantify and measure security assurance as well as improve cloud security.

I also worked on the automation and security of connected vehicle technology and platform tools for embedded and mobile devices.

In my free time, you’ll find me betwixt and between—either playing soccer or running, whether in the jungle or on the treadmill. Oh—and not to mention, I’m a bit of a bookaholic too!
